Overview

Mastır Enerji is specialized in Power Plant equipment manufacturing and erection, and construction works. The company was established in 2001 by a team, with over 30 years of experience, and since then completed numerous projects for the biggest power plants in Turkey. To date we have erected more than 30 units of HRSGs of various types, 24 units of Gas Turbines, and 17 units of Steam Turbines with outputs ranging up to 65MW.
