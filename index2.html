Who We Are?

Mastır Enerji is specialized in Power Plant equipment manufacturing and erection, and construction works. The company was established in 2001 by a team of engineers and technicians with over 30 years of experience, and since then completed numerous projects for the biggest power plants in Turkey. To date we have erected numerious HRSGs, Gas Turbines, and Steam Turbines with outputs ranging up to 65MW.
