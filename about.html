Overview of Mastır Enerji

Mastır Energy Ltd. has started to perform at 2001 to be active in construction, manufacturing and erection works. Our company has the privilege to be the one which has erected gas turbines, steam turbines, HRSGs at most numbers within its first six years. Other than erecting natural gas burning power plants, our company has the Certificate of Construction Service from EMRA (Energy Market Regulatory Authority / EPDK: Enerji Piyasası Düzenleme Kurumu), and has completed many constructions of natural gas distribution pipelines projects.

Mastır Enerji has completed many projects within such short time periods which may be considered as references, by acting within certain quality policy, it holds the certificates of ISO 9001:2008, EN 3834-2, PED 97/23/EC, also achieved the certification of ASME “A” & “S” Stamps.

Quality Policy

Mastır Enerji has accepted items below as its Quality Policy;

  • To manufacture within the designated standards by using the latest technology
  • To hold the customer satisfaction on the top, to solve any complains coming from our customer within the shortest time period and effective way without any concession.
  • To be influential in global market by using the sources effectively
  • To apply Quality System Conditions and to better off its effectiveness all the time
  • To follow the technological progresses with professional team work

Personnel Structure

Variety of our staff follows the sectoral changes on continual bases, and the structure of staff changes itself to reflect the new technological progresses to the fields of activities. The staff is entrepreneur, able to take initiative, experienced and also has the flexibility to adapt themselves to the dynamic and changing conditions.They can work mobilized whenever needed and company charges its staff personnel according to the dimensions and properties of the project the best and appropriate way.

Fields of Activities

Mastır Enerji has been organized to work in Turkey and Foreign Countries in the fields of constructing solid, liquid and gas fuel burning power plants, erection of gas and steam turbines, combined cycle plants,co-generation systems, tanks designed for various purposes.

Under Scope of Mechanical Works:

  • Erection of Combined Cycle Power Plants
  • Erection of Thermal Power Plants
  • Erection of Hydro Electric Power Plants
  • Erection of Refineries and other Industrial Plants
  • Manufacturing and Installation of Pipelines, Steel Structures and Tanks
  • Continuous and Periodical Maintenance of Industrial Plants
  • QA/QC Consultancy Services
  • Control Services for materials and welds with Destructive or Non-destructive Methods
  • Stress Relief Services for pipe welds
  • Thermal & Acoustic Insulation Works

Under Scope of Electrical and Instrumentation Works

  • Installation of Facilities and Equipments
  • Process Control And Instrumentation
  • Power Distributing Lines and Transformer Buildings
  • H.V. L.V. Power Switchyards
  • Power Distributing Panels
  • Calibration of Instruments
